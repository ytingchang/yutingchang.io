Easter Egg

1. Pixar Ball

Also known as the Luxo, Jr. ball, in Luca, it literally takes an eagle eyed viewer to find it. That’s because it’s stuck on a roof in Portorosso, only seen during a quick flyover when the bike race begins.

2. Pizza Planet Truck

The Pizza Planet truck, a truck that is used to make deliveries for Pizza Planet in Toy Story, appears in the form of a Piaggio Ape (a three-wheeled scooter wagon) parked on a street in Portorosso.

3. Zip Code

The number at the front of the train is 96408, the postal code for Emeryville, California. That is also where Pixar's headquarter is located.

4. 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea

Set in the mid-century, it’s no wonder that the local movie theater in Portorosso is playing the latest Walt Disney Pictures release, 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea. When Luca participates in the swimming competition, he wears a Jules Verne-style diving suit to hide his seamonster identity, just like the suits worn in the 1954 classic.
